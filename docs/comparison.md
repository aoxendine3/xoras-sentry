### Why Schema-Based Detection Matters
While every serious scanner uses AST analysis, XORAS Sentry goes a step further by implementing **Schema-Based Hallucination Detection**. 

Standard scanners look for patterns that "look like" keys. XORAS cross-references your actual `process.env` calls against your authoritative project schema. If your code is accessing an environment variable that hasn't been declared, we catch it instantly. This is the only way to solve configuration drift and the risks of AI-generated code.
