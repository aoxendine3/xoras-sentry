## 2. Strategic Rationale
* **Directness:** Respects the founder/executive's time by leading with flat-fee pricing and concrete, rapid timelines (48 hours).
* **Value-Oriented:** Emphasizes privacy (zero-trust, fully local sandboxing) and hardware-bound JWS sovereignty over performative sales speak.
* **Low Friction Call-to-Action (CTA):** Asking to share a "sample report" rather than demanding a calendar call lowers response resistance.
